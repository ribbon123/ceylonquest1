Special Tours

Beyond our popular travel destinations, we also offer a range of specialized tours designed to immerse you in Sri Lanka’s extraordinary natural and cultural heritage. These handpicked experiences bring you closer to the island’s rare wildlife, rich history, and timeless traditions.

Whale Wonders

Set sail on the turquoise waters and witness the majestic blue whales, playful dolphins, and other marine life up close.

Our guided tours ensure a safe, responsible, and awe-inspiring encounter with the ocean’s gentle giants.

Bird Life Safaris

Sri Lanka is a paradise for bird lovers, home to over 400 species including many endemics.

Explore lush rainforests, wetlands, and sanctuaries as our expert guides help you spot vibrant kingfishers, hornbills, eagles, and other rare species.

Turtle Trails

Visit protected nesting beaches where sea turtles come ashore to lay their eggs.

Learn about conservation efforts and witness one of nature’s most magical moments while supporting eco-friendly tourism.

Ancient Kingdom Exploration

Journey through Sri Lanka’s glorious past with visits to sacred temples, ancient kingdoms, and UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

Explore wonders like Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa, Sigiriya, and Kandy as you discover the island’s rich cultural and spiritual history.
