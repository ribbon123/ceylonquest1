Destinations

At Ceylon Quest, we bring you the very best of Sri Lanka from beautiful sandy beaches to breathtaking wildlife encounters and adrenaline-filled adventures. Whether you're dreaming of a relaxing getaway or an action-packed journey, our curated travel experiences are designed to help you discover the island’s true beauty.

Paradise Beaches

Feel the warm tropical breeze as you stroll along Sri Lanka’s pristine coastlines. From the surfing paradise of Arugam Bay to the golden sands of Unawatuna, we guide you to the most stunning shores where you can relax, surf, or simply enjoy the sunset.

Trincomalee

Wide stretches of white, ivory sand, calm sea, and lovely palm trees.

Very popular for snorkeling and diving nearby Pigeon Island National Park offers rich marine life, including coral reefs.

Hikkaduwa

Southwest coast known for its golden sands, clear water, and vibrant coral reef close to shore.

Best place for snorkelling, with easy chances to see tropical fish and sea turtles.

Unawatuna

Calm turquoise waters, soft golden sand, and lush palm fringes perfect for swimming and sunbathing.

Surrounded by cafés, restaurants and accommodation close by good for a comfortable beach holiday.

Arugam Bay

A laid-back, rustic vibe good for surfers, backpackers, or those wanting a more “remote” beach experience

Great if you enjoy adventure, nature, and a less touristy beach life.

Wildlife Discovery

Sri Lanka is a wildlife lover’s dream. Explore world-famous national parks such as Yala, Wilpattu, and Minneriya, where you can witness elephants, leopards, exotic birds, and countless other species in their natural habitats. Our tours ensure safe, ethical, and unforgettable wildlife adventures.

Wilpattu

The largest national park in Sri Lanka which offers a peaceful experience.

Excellent for spotting leopards, elephants, and rare birds.

Yala

Famous for having one of the highest densities of leopards in the world and great destination for safari lovers.

Home to elephants, sloth bears, crocodiles, and a huge variety of birds.

Udawalawe

One of the best places in Asia to see wild elephants up close.

Popular for safari tours, especially family-friendly trips.

Sinharaja

The island’s last primary tropical rainforest, full of rare rainforest species.

Lush, green paradise for birdwatchers: Sri Lanka blue magpie, red-faced malkoha, and more.

Adventure Tours

For thrill-seekers, Sri Lanka offers an endless playground like up ancient peaks, go white-water rafting. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced adventurer, we help you craft the perfect adventure itinerary.

Ella

Nestled in the misty highlands, Ella is a haven of breathtaking scenery and peaceful mountain vibes

Hike to Ella Rock for sweeping views, wander through lush tea plantations, and marvel at the iconic Nine Arches Bridge.

Kitulgala

Known as Sri Lanka’s adventure capital Kitulgala is ideal for thrill-seekers with unforgettable scenery.

Experience white-water rafting along the Kelani River, trek through rainforests, or explore mysterious caves hidden among the wilderness.

Worlds End (Horton Plains)

Located in the serene Horton Plains National Park, World’s End offers one of the most dramatic cliff drops in Asia.

The panoramic views stretching across misty mountains and deep valleys make it a must-visit for hikers. Witness the stunning sunrise as the clouds part beneath your feet.

Bentota

Famous for its golden beaches and calm waters, Bentota is a paradise for beach lovers and water-sports enthusiasts.

Enjoy jet skiing, snorkeling, river safaris along the Bentota River, or simply unwind on peaceful shores. It’s the ideal destination for both adventure and relaxation.
